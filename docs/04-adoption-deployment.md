# 04 - Adoption and Deployment

## Deployment principles

- Deploy in phases with measurable outcomes.
- Prioritize identity, endpoint, and data controls by risk.
- Keep change management visible and role-specific.

## 90-day deployment model

### Days 1-15: Foundation

- Confirm scope, assumptions, and dependencies
- Establish implementation governance
- Complete baseline and quick-win hardening

### Days 16-45: Pilot and validation

- Pilot key controls with representative users/workloads
- Measure usability and operational impact
- Tune policies and support workflows

### Days 46-75: Scale rollout

- Expand deployment in planned waves
- Run targeted user communications and enablement
- Track adoption metrics and service desk patterns

### Days 76-90: Stabilize and optimize

- Validate KPI movement against baseline
- Complete handoff and operating procedures
- Agree optimization backlog and roadmap

## Adoption workstream

- Executive sponsor briefings every 2 weeks
- Admin enablement and runbook walk-throughs
- End-user awareness campaigns aligned to policy changes
- Reinforcement checkpoints at day 30, 60, and 90

## Risks and mitigations

- Risk: Policy friction reduces adoption.
  - Mitigation: staged pilot, rapid feedback loops, and exceptions workflow.
- Risk: Customer team bandwidth is limited.
  - Mitigation: clear RACI and partner-led execution blocks.
- Risk: Value not visible to leadership.
  - Mitigation: monthly KPI narrative tied to business outcomes.
