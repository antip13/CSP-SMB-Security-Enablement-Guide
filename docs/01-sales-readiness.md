# 01 - Sales Readiness

## Readiness goals

- Position SMB security urgency with business relevance.
- Qualify opportunities using risk, compliance, and operational indicators.
- Guide customers from problem framing to solution confidence.

## Sales readiness checklist

- [ ] Team can articulate SMB security market pressure and threat trends.
- [ ] Team has a standard discovery script and qualification rubric.
- [ ] Team has customer-ready pitch materials for core scenarios.
- [ ] Team has an objection handling matrix for budget and complexity concerns.
- [ ] Team can explain integrated value of Microsoft 365 Business Premium + advanced suites.

## Discovery conversation flow

1. Business context and growth priorities
2. Security incidents, current controls, and risk exposure
3. Compliance/data protection expectations
4. IT capacity constraints and partner support requirements
5. Prioritized outcomes for the next 90 days

## Qualification rubric (simple score)

- Risk urgency (0-5)
- Compliance pressure (0-5)
- Endpoint/identity complexity (0-5)
- Executive sponsorship (0-5)
- Timeline urgency (0-5)

Use thresholds:

- 20+: high-priority motion
- 12-19: nurture with targeted workshops
- <12: education-first motion

## Common objections and response themes

- "We are too small for advanced security."
  - Response: SMBs are frequent targets; integrated controls reduce operational overhead.
- "This seems complex to deploy."
  - Response: phased deployment model with partner-led onboarding and quick-win milestones.
- "Budget is constrained."
  - Response: prioritize highest-risk workloads first and align spend to measurable risk reduction.

## Sales assets to maintain

- Account discovery template
- Customer pitch deck by scenario
- Proposal statement of work skeleton
- Success story snippets and references
