# 00 - Overview

## Purpose

Build a repeatable CSP motion to help SMB customers improve security posture using Microsoft 365 Business Premium and advanced security/compliance add-ons.

## Outcomes

- Higher security-led pipeline quality
- Faster time from discovery to proposal
- Better win rate on security-led opportunities
- Stronger post-sale adoption and retention

## Program pillars

1. Sales readiness: consistent messaging, qualification, and objection handling.
2. Offer packaging: clear bundles, pricing logic, and expected outcomes.
3. Scenario execution: role-based playbooks for common SMB needs.
4. Adoption and deployment: prescriptive onboarding and value realization.
5. Operational excellence: KPI tracking and continuous optimization.

## Team roles

- Sales lead: owns pipeline strategy and stage progression.
- Solution specialist: leads discovery and maps business risk to capabilities.
- Technical architect: validates design, deployment, and security baseline.
- Customer success manager: drives user adoption and measurable outcomes.

## Cadence

- Weekly: pipeline and scenario review
- Bi-weekly: proposal quality review
- Monthly: adoption outcomes and KPI trends
- Quarterly: offer refresh and content update
