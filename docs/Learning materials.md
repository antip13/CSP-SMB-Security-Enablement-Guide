# Lerning materials and playbooks
---
This playbook is designed for Microsoft partners and Managed Service Providers seeking to build, scale, and operationalise a repeatable, tiered managed security and compliance offering for Microsoft 365 Business Premium customers.
---
The DEFEND framework is a partner-delivered service construct, not a Microsoft SKU.
Its purpose is to simplify customer conversations, standardise service delivery, and enable predictable commercial outcomes, while remaining fully aligned with Microsoft licensing and product boundaries
